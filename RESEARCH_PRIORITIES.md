# Kleptocracy Timeline Research Priorities

## Overview
This document outlines research priorities for documenting institutional capture across different decades and capture lanes. Each priority includes specific events, networks, and mechanisms to investigate.

## Capture Lanes Definition
- **Regulatory Capture**: Control of regulatory agencies by the industries they oversee
- **Judicial Capture**: Influence over courts and legal systems
- **Legislative Capture**: Control of lawmakers and legislative processes
- **Media Capture**: Control of information flow and public narrative
- **Electoral Capture**: Manipulation of democratic processes
- **Intelligence Capture**: Privatization and misuse of intelligence capabilities
- **Financial Capture**: Control of monetary systems and financial oversight
