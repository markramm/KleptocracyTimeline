First comprehensive inter-organizational legal resistance coalition meeting, bringing together over 650 organizations including ACLU, Protect Democracy, Brennan Center, and Democracy 2025. The coalition, representing 2000+ legal experts, strategizes coordinated legal approaches to defend democratic institutions against potential authoritarian threats. Key focus areas include challenging executive overreach, protecting civil liberties, defending vulnerable communities, and utilizing proactive litigation strategies.
