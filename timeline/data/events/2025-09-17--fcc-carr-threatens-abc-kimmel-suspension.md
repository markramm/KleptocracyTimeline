Hours later, Disney suspended 'Jimmy Kimmel Live' indefinitely. Republican Senators Ted Cruz and Rand Paul condemned Carr's threats as 'dangerous' and 'absolutely inappropriate,' with Cruz comparing them to Mafia tactics. The ACLU stated Trump officials were 'abusing their power to stop ideas they don't like,' calling it 'a grave threat to our First Amendment freedoms.'
