J.D. Vance sworn in as 50th Vice President at age 40, completing transformation from Hillbilly Elegy author and Trump critic to second-highest office, backed by Peter Thiel network influence. Became the third-youngest vice president in U.S. history, first Millennial and first Marine Corps veteran to hold the office. Sworn in by Supreme Court Justice Brett Kavanaugh during an indoor ceremony in the Capitol Rotunda due to freezing temperatures.
