EPA Administrator Lee Zeldin announces 31 historic deregulation actions in "the greatest and most consequential day of deregulation in U.S. history," including reconsidering the 2009 greenhouse gas endangerment finding. Chemical industry veterans Nancy Beck (American Chemistry Council) and Lynn Dekleva (DuPont/American Chemistry Council) now oversee chemical safety regulations.
