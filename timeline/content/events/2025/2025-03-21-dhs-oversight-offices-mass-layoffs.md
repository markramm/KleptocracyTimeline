Over 100 CRCL employees lost their jobs. DHS spokesperson called these statutorily-required offices "internal adversaries" that "obstructed immigration enforcement." The cuts occurred as 11 people died in ICE custody since FY2025 began. By May, only a handful of executives remained, unable to perform mandated oversight functions.
