Paramount Global agreed to pay $16 million to President Donald Trump's presidential library to settle a lawsuit over a '60 Minutes' pre-election interview with Kamala Harris. The settlement includes no apology and appears to be strategically timed to support Paramount's pending merger with Skydance Media.
