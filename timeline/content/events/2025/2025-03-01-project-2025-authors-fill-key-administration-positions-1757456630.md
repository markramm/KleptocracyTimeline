An in-depth analysis reveals that over 70% of Trump's cabinet members have direct connections to the Project 2025 blueprint, with more than 50 high-level officials from the project strategically placed in key government roles. Notable appointees include Brendan Carr (FCC Chairman), Tom Homan (Border Czar), and Linda McMahon (Education Secretary), who have either authored or closely aligned with Project 2025's comprehensive governmental transformation strategy.
