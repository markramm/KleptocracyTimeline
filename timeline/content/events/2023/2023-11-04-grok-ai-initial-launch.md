On November 4, 2023, xAI, Elon Musk's AI company, initiated the preview release of Grok, an AI chatbot designed to compete with ChatGPT. Grok is integrated with X (formerly Twitter) and aims to provide more direct, unfiltered responses compared to other AI systems. The initial release was limited to select X Premium users, marking a significant moment in the competitive AI chatbot market. The launch represents Musk's effort to create an alternative to existing AI chatbots with real-time knowledge from the X platform.
