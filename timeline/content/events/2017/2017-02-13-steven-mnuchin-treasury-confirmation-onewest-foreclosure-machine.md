Steven Mnuchin confirmed as Treasury Secretary despite Democrats labeling him operator of "foreclosure machine" through OneWest Bank. After 17 years at Goldman Sachs (partner 1994, mortgage desk head), acquired failed IndyMac in 2009, rebranding as OneWest. Bank executed over 36,000 foreclosures under his leadership, accounting for 39% of all reverse mortgage foreclosures post-housing collapse. Engaged in robo-signing, document fraud, and predatory practices including foreclosing on 90-year-old woman over 27-cent error. California AG memo documented "widespread misconduct" including backdating documents and failing to provide adequate foreclosure notice.
