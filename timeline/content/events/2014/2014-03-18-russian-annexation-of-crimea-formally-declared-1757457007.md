President Vladimir Putin signed a treaty formally incorporating Crimea into the Russian Federation, following a disputed referendum held under Russian military occupation. The international community widely condemned the annexation as a violation of international law, including the UN Charter, the Helsinki Final Act, and multiple bilateral treaties.
